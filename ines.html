OMNIPROFILE


Licensed Contemporary PUP Artist, Surface Designer, children's Illustrator/Author, Kim Niles published her first book in 2005. Shortly thereafter, her KiniArt™ company was licensed and it has been Niles' full-time job for more than a decade. Kim sells her original art online at Etsy & Facebook, and at Garry Oak Gallery (Oak Harbor, WA). Niles also licenses her designs to other companies for their products, and runs several online KiniArt™ shops offering products from fabric to home decor featuring her unique designs. The majority of her studio time, is spent on small scale illustrations on paper, such as the ones featured in her children's books, but she also enjoys painting with mixed media acrylics on canvas, and accepts custom Petcature™ Portrait art orders. KiniArt is collected internationally. Niles' children's books are found on Amazon, B&N, and can be ordered through your local bookstore. Kim lives on the beautiful Whidbey Island in WA state, and enjoys working on art for license, custom portraits, fabric designs, drawing whatever animals are taking over her mind, and working on her next children's book as time permits.
